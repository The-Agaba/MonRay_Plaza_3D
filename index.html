MonRay Housing Project — build notes

Rebuilt directly from your labelled map
  • Plot: 101.5 ft long × 10 ft deep, with a continuous 6 ft open canopy along the road-facing front, matching your legend (open canopy = no outer wall, just posts + roof).
  • Layout left→right: Duka 1 (12ft), Duka 2 (12ft), Duka 3 (10ft), Large Duka (13.5ft, single roll-up gate + its own public toilet) — then House 2 — Living Room (10ft, single exterior entrance door) + Bed Room (12ft, private toilet/bath) — then House 1 — Bedroom (12ft, private toilet/bath) + Living Room (10ft, single exterior entrance door). Viewed from the road, shops read left, houses read right.
  • Each house has exactly one exterior door (its Living Room). All other rooms in that house — the bedroom, the toilet/bath — are reached through interior doors, so they're private and self-contained, per your instruction.
  • Two privacy divider walls run the full 6 ft canopy depth: one between the Large Duka and House 1's porch, one between House 1 and House 2's porches. Toggle them from the "Canopy Privacy Walls" button — everywhere else the canopy stays fully open between bays.
  • Doors now swing on a real hinge (or roll up, for the shop gates) — click a door/gate in View or Walk mode, or press E while close to one in Walk mode, to open or close it.
  • Everything — walls, doors, windows, dimensions — is still editable in Edit Plan mode if anything needs correcting.
